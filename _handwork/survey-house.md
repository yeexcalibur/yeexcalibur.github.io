### Survey ·Daofu County in Ganzi Tibetan Autonomous Prefecture 甘孜·道孚建筑测绘 2014

Regarding the architectural surveying and mapping of Daofu County in Ganzi Tibetan Autonomous Prefecture, this work records the earthquake-resistant wooden structural joints (Bengke & Bengle) and the architectural characteristics of local buildings.<br>
关于甘孜道孚的建筑测绘，记录其抗震木结构节点（崩科&崩勒）及建筑特点。
