**Role**: Lead designer / consultant  
**Scope**: Industrial building degign, smart-port, energy consumption and equipment optimization, synergy between comfort and safety<br>
**Year**: 2024–2026  

### Overview
Through precise positioning and technological innovation, the two types of buildings can achieve functional complementarity, form an industrial ecological closed loop of "office + warehousing", and maximize land value and return on investment (ROI).

### Highlights
- 多维协同规划：结合道路与使用功能，实施架空线入地工程，提升安全性与景观环境，促进土地集约利用与城市空间优化。Coordinated road alignment and site functions with underground cable installation, enhancing safety, landscape quality, and land-use efficiency.
- 低碳港口理念：以绿色集约为核心，通过BIM与节能模拟优化建筑与堆场布局，容积率达1.27，建筑系数68.57%，树立土地高效利用标杆。Applied BIM and energy simulation to achieve compact, energy-efficient layout (FAR 1.27, building coverage 68.57%), setting a benchmark for land efficiency.
- 多式联运创新：融合铁路、公路、水运、空运体系，构建“公铁水空”立体运输网络，提升港口运营效率与物流连通性。Integrated rail, road, water, and air logistics systems to establish a comprehensive “intermodal transport network,” enhancing port competitiveness.
- 政策与产业协同：纳入政府重点推进项目，助力构建“智慧港口+低碳物流”示范体系，强化枢纽经济的区域引领作用。Recognized as a key government-supported project promoting the “Smart Port + Low-Carbon Logistics” model, reinforcing regional hub economy development.

### My Job<br>
### Design + Consulting | Lead deginer
- Led overall master planning, spatial organization, architectural design and full-cycle consulting assessment for office, warehouse and yard complexes. 
- Advanced the 'Smart Port + Low-Carbon Logistics' initiative through BIM integration and energy modeling to achieve sustainability targets.
