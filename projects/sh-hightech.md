**Role**: Lead designer<br>
**Scope**: Green building design, energy efficiency analysis, BIM coordination, low-carbon architecture, sustainable industrial design, daylight optimization, cleanroom building design, energy simulation report, façade system optimization<br>
**Year**: 2022–2023  

### Overview
Occupies a 66,000 m² mixed-use site integrating office, commercial, and light industrial functions. It includes administrative and R&D buildings, clean-lab facilities, and manufacturing plants, forming a high-tech incubation and production hub that supports research, innovation, and industrial transformation. 
The masterplan emphasizes sustainable development, energy efficiency, and spatial synergy to advance Songjiang’s industrial upgrading agenda.<br>
**Shanghai Municipal Major Project 2023**

### Highlights
- 功能复合与产业协同：将办公、研发与生产功能集约布局，形成“研发孵化—中试验证—产业转化”完整链条。The masterplan combines office, R&D, and production zones into a compact, efficient layout, establishing a full “research–pilot–production” innovation chain.
- 绿色建筑与节能设计：项目严格执行国内《绿色建筑评价标准》要求，洁净实验楼采用节能幕墙系统与自然采光优化设计，建筑性能卓越。In compliance with China’s Green Building Evaluation Standard, the clean-lab buildings adopt energy-saving façades and optimized daylight systems for high-performance operation.
- 智慧园区规划：通过BIM协同设计与能源模拟技术优化建筑体量及设备配置，提高建筑能效比与维护可控性。BIM coordination and energy simulation technologies enhance spatial efficiency and system reliability.
- 产业升级示范：作为上海松江区重点推进项目，项目建成后将成为区域高新技术产业发展的示范节点。As a key project in Songjiang District, it serves as a model for sustainable high-tech industrial development and regional innovation.

### My Job
### Lead deginer
- Responsible for conceptual and schematic design, pushing forward site master planning and building design development.
- Collaborated with the team on overall park planning and design refinement through analytical studies and drawing optimization.
- Independently led the clean-lab buildings (Blocks 5 & 6) and partial pilot plants and utility station design during the preliminary design phase.
- Conducted energy performance analysis in compliance with China’s Green Building Standard and completed official energy calculation reports for review submission.
