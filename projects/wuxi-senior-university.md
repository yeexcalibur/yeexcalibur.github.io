**Role**: Lead designer / consultant  
**Scope**: Facade, entrance, interior, landscape, MEP upgrade  
**Year**: 2023–2025  

### Overview
Pilot renovation shifting from barrier-free basics to **cognitive activation** via spatial and programmatic design.
Listed among the 2025 People’s Livelihood Projects.

### Highlights
- 门头与立面更新，强化识别与归属感 Renovation of doorways and facades to enhance recognition and a sense of belonging 
- 公共空间“认知刺激”路径：颜色、地标、材料、社交节点 "Cognitive stimulation" paths in public spaces: color, landmarks, materials, social nodes 
- 低眩光照明、分区控制、景观环境治理 Low-glare lighting, zoned control, and landscape environment improvement

### My Job<br>
### Design + Consulting | Project Leader 
-	Spearheaded architectural façade, interior, and landscape redesigns to align with future exhibition and operational goals. 
-	Proposed the concept of “Cognitive Enhancement Design”, integrating sensory guidance to enhance learning and social interaction for elderly users.
-	Directed end-to-end consulting assessments and delivered age-inclusive renovation strategies improving user experience.
